Contact Extension - JavaScript Hooks
====================================
1. Person
	contact.person.saved
	@param	{Number} idPerson
	Evoked after person saved. (DB record updated, person elements updated in DOM)



2. Company
	contact.company.saved
	@param	{Number} idCompany
	Evoked after company saved. (DB record updated, company elements updated in DOM)



3. Panelwdiget Stafflist
	panelwidget.stafflist.onPersonClick
	@param	{Number} idPerson
	Evoked after person selected from stafflist.
